How to Play

Click 'Load Puzzle' to start the game. The puzzle will load onto the grid and the timer will start. The grid is checked as you fill it in. It will show red on any incorrect value. When you finish the puzzle, the game will end and your final score will be displayed. Your score depends on your speed and accuracy. Have fun!
